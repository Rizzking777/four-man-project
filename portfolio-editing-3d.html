Modern House Exterior Design

A custom 3D exterior visualization created for an individual client to help visualize the final appearance of their future home before construction.

The project highlights a modern minimalist facade featuring a combination of wood textures, stone accents, and contemporary architectural elements. Detailed modeling and realistic presentation allow the client to review the design concept, proportions, and overall visual harmony with confidence.

Services included concept visualization, 3D modeling, exterior detailing, and presentation rendering using SketchUp.
